How WeTeach Works

Our platform makes it easy to connect teachers and learners with a simple process

For Learners

1. Create Hiring Post

Describe your learning needs and requirements

2. Teachers Apply

Qualified teachers will express interest

3. Select a Teacher

Choose the teacher that best fits your needs

4. Get Matched

Connect and start learning right away

For Teachers

1. Create Profile

Showcase your expertise and experience

2. Browse Posts

Find teaching opportunities that match your skills

3. Apply for Posts

Submit your application to interested learners

4. Get Selected

Connect with students and start teaching

Direct Request Flow: View Teacher Profile → Perform Teaching Request → Teacher Responds
